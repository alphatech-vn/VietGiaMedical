# Support

VietGia Medical ERP is an internal business system.

## Support routing

Support should be handled through:

- the internal deployment owner,
- or an authorized system administrator.

## What to include when asking for help

Please include:

- the module involved,
- what role you are using,
- what action failed,
- screenshots if the issue is visual,
- any project or task context relevant to the issue.

## Sensitive note

Do not post or publish:

- internal credentials,
- project financial data,
- customer records,
- tender details,
- internal personnel data.
