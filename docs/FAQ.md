# FAQ

## What is VietGia Medical ERP?

It is an internal management system for tracking sales projects, bidding progress, tasks, products, personnel, and reporting in a medical-device business context.

## Who is it for?

Primary users include:

- executive leadership,
- sales managers,
- product managers,
- sales staff,
- system administrators.

## Is this a public SaaS product?

No. It appears to be an internal operational system rather than a public self-service SaaS offering.

## What does it manage?

- project pipeline,
- tender and procurement progress,
- customers and dealers,
- product and product-group context,
- tasks and personnel,
- reporting and analytics.

## Where can I understand the system structure?

Start with:

- `https://viet-gia-medical-guideline.vercel.app/`

## Does this repository contain the source code?

No. This is a public product-information repository only.
